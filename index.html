Chaos Lords Support Page

Q1: How do I unlock buildings at the base?

You need to level up your account, all the buildings unlock at level 10.

Q2: How can I improve my gear?

You can enhance it at the Forge.

Q3: Where can I get hourglasses for raiding missions?

You can buy them at the market or find in a Time Chest (also sold at the market).

Q4: Why can't I fight World Bosses?

You need to join a guild first.

Q5: How can I unlock new heroes?

You can get them for winning missions and in the market.
